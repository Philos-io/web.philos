Why Philos?

State of the art curriculum

We focus on bleeding edge technologies that will stay for years. We saw the potential of JavaScript years ago, we were right. JavaScript has gone big and is growing bigger every day/it is even becoming the norm these days. From IoT to real-time interactive apps ; from back end to front end - JavaScript covers it all. Don't miss the bus.

Amazing instructors

Our instructors are software engineers and passionate developers who love to teach and who are incredibly good at it. They have embraced JavaScript years ago and have been lead engineers in top companies as well as in startups. They will provide you with what you need to raise your skills to the next level.

Hands-on - Project based

We are strong believers in the learning by doing. This is why our workshops are mostly hands-on and project based. You will get to work in pairs, experiment and hack the latest technologies to truly experience and understand the language you will be working on.

High quality matters

The hours spent at Philos are neuron-intensive, high quality hours. We always aim for small teams to foster communication between participants and encourage interactions with instructors. We've built an ultra pertinent curriculum thanks to our amazing community and the invaluable feedback from former participants.

Immersive environment

What we offer you at Philos is an immersive environment with passionate developers like you who have a thirst for knowledge. With our events, we encourage our former students and the tech community to meet, talk and dream about tech. Coming at Philos will be like going to Rome to learn Italian, a fully immersive experience.

Top students & great community

Philo's workshops are intense. Students attending them are passionate and dedicated to improving their coding skills. Even after the course's normal hours, most of our students find themselves staying much longer to talk about the future of technology or the last feature they've added to their project.

Make the choice that will put you on the fast track. Stay ahead of the game.
