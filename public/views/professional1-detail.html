What to look forward to? (per month)

1 HackJam

HackJams take place on the 2nd Wednesday of each month, at 7PM.
Here's how it works:

  • - 30 minutes of introduction on the chosen topic
  • - 2 hours of pair programming on practical examples
  • - 30 minutes debating and demoing the solutions teams came up with

3 Open Source Sessions

Open Source Sessions happen on

  • - Saturday morning
  • - Tuesday evening
  • - Thursday evening

They are informal gatherings where developers get together to work on open source projects. It's the perfect way to share, challenge and learn from each others' experience. They generally last 3 to 4 hours

WHAT IF I SIGN UP AND WANT TO CANCEL?

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your subscription will remain active until the current time period is finished.

HOW DOES THE BILLING WORK?

We send you an invoice by email. If you chose the annual subscription, you will only receive one invoice for the whole year. If you chose the monthly subscrition, you will receive one every month.
