WHY IS IT RELEVANT FOR YOU?

This fast tracked learning experience will take you from zero skills to being able to code your own application in a single day.

Our goal is for you to acquire a clear and compelling view that transform your discernment of the Digital World. Through a better understanding of the concepts behind the coding languages, the workflow and the infrastructure that make the digital world, you will have all the tools to navigate seamlessly in any digital environment.

For businesses, an environment of digital awareness enriches the reflections with constructive and creative discussions about technology.

For individuals, understanding the technological advancements opens the door to a whole new world of opportunities. Digitally-aware individuals have this ability to use the technological tools around them to participate to shaping the future.

Crack the code is especially fitted for persons dealing with IT on a regular basis. In particular: IT project/product managers, business managers, online marketing related functions, e-business, HR/recruiters in an IT sector.
